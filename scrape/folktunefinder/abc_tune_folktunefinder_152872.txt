C:anon., words: Petter Dass
O:Norway
N:The priest and poet Petter Dass (1647-1707) is a legendary, nearly
N:mythological figure in Northen Norway. In these verses he complains
N:bitterly about the lousy treatment he got while visititing some of
N:the - let's say rustic - people in the country. Neither the food nor
N:the bedding was to his liking. I have more or less kept the original
N:spelling which is rather different from modern Norwegian.
